about the journey

I was an iPod kid. At some point, I got an iPod shuffle and later a library card, and you can guess the rest of the story.

My shuffle has survived many camping trips, quite a few bus/car rides, and one or two cycles in the wash. My plan is to create what I've always wanted, a little Minecraft-themed musical travel companion.

As I continue to develop and refine this project, I may consider selling some revisions or limited edition versions of the jukebox. The goal is a fully functional model with many toggleable features.

If you're interested in learning more about potential sales or collaborations, please don't hesitate to reach out!
